ChatAfrica answers ALL your questions about Africa. It is a text-based chatbot web application, that provides you with in-depth information about African people, their history and diverse cultures. It is independent of locale and allows for global usage.


ChatAfrica is SUPER easy to use.

Get all the information you need about Africa with just a few clicks. Try it out now! :)

Get Started

Examples

  • "What is the capital of Kenya?"
  • "How far back can you tell of African history?"
  • "Why should we study african history?"

Capabilities

  • Remembers what user said earlier in the conversation
  • Allows user to provide follow-up corrections
  • Trained to decline inappropriate requests

Limitations

  • May occasionally generate incorrect information
  • May occasionally produce harmful instructions or biased content
  • Limited knowledge of world and events after 2022
